## Critical Finding: Travelpayouts Marker is a Placeholder

**Line 1554:** `const TP_MARKER = "YOUR_TP_MARKER";`

The `buildFlightUrl()` function at line 1558-1568 checks if TP_MARKER is set. Since it's the placeholder string, the condition `TP_MARKER !== "YOUR_TP_MARKER"` is false, so ALL flight links go directly to `aviasales.com/search/...` — no `tp.media` redirect, no affiliate tracking, no commission.

The Travelpayouts API is live (fetching real prices through the VPS proxy), but the click-through links earn nothing. This is the single biggest revenue leak.

**Fix:** Jack needs to log into tp.media, grab the marker string, and replace `"YOUR_TP_MARKER"` at line 1554. Five-minute fix worth potentially $0.14+ RPM from day one.

### What's Working Well
- **Sticky bottom bar with Flights + Hotels is excellent UX.** Always visible, clear pricing, Plausible tracking on flight clicks. This is the #1 conversion surface.
- **Booking.com integration is solid.** Dates pass through from search filters, location pre-fills, and the affiliate ID is present. Two touchpoints (sticky bar + dedicated card) maximize exposure.

- **SafetyWing placement after hotels is logical** — user is already thinking about travel logistics.

### What Needs Work
- **Flight CTA is the most prominent button in the app but earns $0.** The dark #222 "Flights · $XXX" button is the first thing users tap. Every click is wasted revenue.
- **REI links are the majority of gear items in 5 categories** (skiing has 4 REI / 2 Amazon, climbing has 4 REI / 2 Amazon, kayak has 4 REI / 2 Amazon, MTB has 2 REI / 2 Backcountry / 2 Amazon, hiking has 4 REI / 2 Amazon). That's 22 REI links earning $0 vs ~20 Amazon links earning commission.

- **No Plausible event on gear clicks, hotel clicks, or SafetyWing clicks.** Only flight clicks are tracked. We're blind to which revenue streams get the most engagement.
- **Duplicate Booking.com touchpoint** (sticky bar + card lower in sheet) — not a problem, but the lower card could be better used for a different monetization partner.

### A/B Test Ideas
1. **Flight CTA copy:** "Flights · $XXX" vs "Book flights from $XXX" vs "See flights ↗"
