### 2. Dev: 3 surgical fixes. 30 minutes total. Ship as one commit.

**a) getFlightDeal fake pricing (3 min)**
`app.jsx:5430–5433` — the `pct = 28 + (seed % 48)` block generates fake 28–75% discounts on all venues before Travelpayouts responds. **c) Email capture backend (20 min)**
`app.jsx:7214` — replace the `alert("You're on the list!")` with a real `fetch` POST to `/api/waitlist` (endpoint exists on VPS). Show inline success state: hide form, show "You're on the list" text. Every email entered since launch is lost. Stop losing them.

After shipping: update CLAUDE.md to accurately reflect what's now fixed. Atomic.

### 3. Dev: venues.json extraction — this week's major engineering task

app.jsx is 2.0 MB. ~1.4 MB is the VENUES array. Babel parses the entire file synchronously before React renders. On 3G/LTE mobile (Reddit/social traffic profile) this is 5–10 seconds of blank screen before any UI appears.
