## Self-improvement note (for Jack, per your standing pref)

You're losing 30+ minutes of context every time you reload `app.jsx` after a multi-edit session and discover a parse error in the browser console. **Save yourself by adding `node -e "require('@babel/parser').parse(require('fs').readFileSync('app.jsx','utf8'),{sourceType:'module',plugins:['jsx']})"` as a pre-push git hook.** Same one-liner that the CI step would run. Catches every double-comma, mismatched brace, and JSX typo before the push leaves your machine. Setup is `git config core.hooksPath .githooks` + a 4-line script. You'll thank yourself by Friday.
