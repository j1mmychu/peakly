## One Product Risk Nobody Is Talking About

**The venue rating/review counts are fabricated and will be noticed.**

Punta Roca has 4,339 reviews on the batch entry and 2,100 on the canonical entry — for the same break. Cloudbreak has 2,980 vs 1,855. These numbers were generated by the data pipeline, not pulled from a real source. An experienced traveler on Reddit will notice that Punta Roca has fewer reviews on Peakly than Yelp, or that the counts changed between two listings for the same spot.

This is not a launch blocker. It becomes a trust blocker at 10K users. The medium-term fix is to either pull real aggregate review data from an API, or drop the review counts entirely and lead with the live conditions score. The conditions score is real. The review count is theater.

**Recommendation:** Delete the count display from VenueCards before the next Reddit post. "4,339 reviews" adds credibility theater today and destroys trust later.
