## CRITICAL ISSUES

### Issue #1: AP_CONTINENT Incomplete (617/776 missing)

**Impact:** HIGH — Breaks continent filtering and affects region-based fallback pricing.

**Affected airports (sample of 617 missing):**
`ACA, ACV, ADA, ADB, ADD, ADL, AQP, ATL, BOS, BCN, BUD, CDG, CUN, DEL, DXB, EDI, FCO, GRX, HKG, IST, JFK, KTM, LAX, LHR, MAD, MIA, MUC, NRT, ORD, PEK, PRG, ROM, SFO, SIN, TKY, TRN, VIE, ZRH...` and many more.

**Current behavior:**
- `getFlightDeal()` defaults `destCont = "europe"` for missing airports
- This causes incorrect fallback pricing for non-European venues
- Continent filter in Alerts tab shows undefined continent labels

**Fix needed:**
Add all 617 missing airports to `AP_CONTINENT` with correct continent codes. Generate from IATA registry or add airports one-by-one to BASE_PRICES entries to trigger discovery.
