## One Product Risk Nobody Is Talking About

**The onboarding sheet collects email, but there is no email list.**

Users who complete onboarding type their email into `profile.email`, which lives in localStorage — on one device, in one browser, and disappears if they clear storage or switch devices. There is no server-side capture. No Mailchimp. No Klaviyo. No webhook. The app has an email field that creates the illusion of a list while capturing nothing.

When the Reddit post goes live and 500 people try the app, some percentage will complete onboarding and enter their email — expecting to receive alerts, updates, or the "Get notified when conditions peak" email capture in the ExploreTab footer. None of them will ever receive anything.

The fix is one `fetch()` call on onboarding complete that POSTs `{email, name, airport}` to a Mailchimp or Loops.so webhook. The VPS is already running. This is a 30-minute implementation. **It should ship before Reddit, not after.**
