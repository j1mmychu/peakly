# Seasonal Venue Analysis - Peakly (April 5, 2026)

## Executive Summary

**Date:** April 5, 2026
**Peak Season Analysis:** Early April marks a critical window in the adventure calendar:
- **Northern Hemisphere:** Late ski season (rapid resort closures mid-month), spring swell season starting, beach season beginning
- **Southern Hemisphere:** Early fall season, excellent surfing conditions, pre-winter ski window closing
- **Tropical/Equatorial:** Peak dry season and optimal tanning conditions

**Key Finding:** Peakly's venue selection is strong for late ski season but **underrepresents Africa and South America** in the beach and surf categories. The app has excellent coverage of North America, Europe, and Indonesia, but is missing key destinations in emerging markets.
