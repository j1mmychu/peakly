## Issues Found

### Critical: Airport Reference Data Gaps

This is the #1 data quality issue in the app.

- **776 unique airport codes** used across venues
- **Only 76 airports** have BASE_PRICES entries (9.8% coverage)
- **Only 179 airports** have AIRPORT_CITY mappings (23.1% coverage)
- **Only 234 airports** have AP_CONTINENT mappings (30.2% coverage)

**Impact:** ~87.6% of venues fall back to estimated flight pricing. The "est." label in the UI handles this gracefully, but real pricing would significantly improve conversion on flight clicks.

**Examples of missing airports:** DRO (Durango), VIL (Vanuatu), ENI (Palawan), FOR (Fortaleza), ACA (Acapulco), ADD (Addis Ababa), ADL (Adelaide)

**Recommendation:** Batch-add the top 100 most-used venue airports to AIRPORT_CITY and AP_CONTINENT. BASE_PRICES expansion is lower priority since the proxy handles real-time lookups.

### Missing Data Fields

- **No elevation field on ski venues.** This prevents accurate late-season scoring. Without elevation, we can't distinguish a 1,200m resort (slushy in March) from a 3,400m resort (still epic). Latitude is a rough proxy but imperfect.
- **No seasonal peak field.** Adding `peakMonths: [12,1,2,3]` to venues would enable much smarter "GO" status and hero card selection.

## Seasonal Picks: Top 5 Venues to Feature This Week

Late March = end of NH ski season, spring surf swells, Caribbean dry season peak, SH autumn opening.

### 1. Uluwatu, Bali — Surfing
Dry season starting. Consistent Indian Ocean swells ramping. Water 28°C. Zero monsoon risk until May. This is the #1 surf destination globally right now.

### 2. Grace Bay Beach, Turks & Caicos — Tanning
Caribbean peak dry season. Air 82°F, water 79°F, zero rain. Turtle nesting season adds a bonus draw.

### 3. Levi, Lapland — Skiing
Arctic latitude (67.8°N) means season extends into April. 20hr daylight. Spring powder. One of the few NH ski destinations still genuinely epic in late March.

### 4. Pipeline, North Shore, Hawaii — Surfing
Tail end of NW swell season. 4-8ft waves, water warming to 78°F. Last chance before swells fade in April. Easter break timing is perfect.

### 5. The Remarkables, Queenstown, NZ — Skiing
Southern Hemisphere ski season literally opening. Fresh snow from autumn fronts. Fewer crowds than peak July-August. Powder hounds from NH should be booking now.

### Venues to Downrank (Off-Season Warnings)
- **Low-altitude NH ski resorts** (below ~2,500m): Colorado front-range resorts, all Vermont/Eastern US ski areas, most Japanese resorts below Hokkaido — slushy to closed by late March
- **Northern European beaches**: Too cold for tanning until June
- **Monsoon-zone venues**: Sri Lanka east coast entering Yala monsoon (April-Sept)

## Decision Made

**Expand AIRPORT_CITY and AP_CONTINENT maps before adding more venues.** The current 2,226 venues are solid, but 83.9% of airports lack city name mappings and 74.6% lack continent mappings. This means flight search UX is degraded for most venues (no city name display, no continent-based alert filtering). Adding the top 100 missing airport codes to these maps is higher ROI than adding more venues. Estimated effort: 2–3 hours dev work, zero API changes needed.
