It's early April. Northern Hemisphere spring is here. Here's what's firing:

1. **Mentawai Islands, Indonesia** (Surfing) — Indian Ocean swell season ramping up. Consistent 6-8ft waves, warm water (28°C), light winds in the morning. Peak season starts now through October.

2. **Whistler Blackcomb, BC** (Skiing) — Spring skiing at its finest. Daytime temps above freezing mean soft corn snow, long days, and fewer crowds than peak winter. Lifts open through late April.

3. **Pipeline, Oahu** (Surfing) — Late-season north swells still hitting. April delivers 4-6ft days with lighter crowds than winter peak. Water temp comfortable at 25°C.
