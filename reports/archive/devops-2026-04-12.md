**Medium-risk items unchanged from yesterday:**

1. **No SRI hashes** on CDN `<script>` tags. Supply-chain risk if unpkg or Sentry CDN is compromised. Fix is trivial — compute sha384 hashes, add `integrity` attributes. I gave you the one-liner yesterday.
2. **No CSP** — Babel Standalone needs `unsafe-eval`, so any CSP will be permissive, but a permissive CSP still blocks most third-party XSS. Ship `default-src 'self'; script-src 'self' 'unsafe-eval' 'unsafe-inline' unpkg.com js.sentry-cdn.com; img-src * data:; style-src 'self' 'unsafe-inline' fonts.googleapis.com; font-src fonts.gstatic.com` as a starting point.
3. **`/api/alerts` is unauthenticated** — anyone can write to it. Low risk today (worker not wired), escalates to medium the moment push is live. Plan for HMAC or origin check.
