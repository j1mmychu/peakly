FINDING #3: Generic Tagging (MEDIUM PRIORITY)
  Status: ACCEPTABLE but IMPROVABLE

  Details:
  - 512 venues (13.7%) have generic, non-differentiating tags
  - Top generic tags:
    * "All Levels" — 340 venues (meaningless difficulty descriptor)
    * "Year-Round" — 302 venues (most venues are year-round)
    * "Family Friendly" — 212 venues (too generic)
    * "Scenic Views" — 103 venues (all adventure venues are scenic)

  Impact:
  - Tag-based filtering works but has low signal
  - Users can't easily distinguish similar venues by tags alone
  - Condition scoring and other features are unaffected

  Why This Doesn't Block Launch:
  - MVP doesn't emphasize tag-based filtering
  - Sorting is by condition score (much stronger signal)
  - Users can still click venues to see details

  Action Plan:
  1. Ship with current tags
  2. Post-Reddit: Audit and replace generic tags
  3. Example improvements:
     * "All Levels" → "Beginner-Advanced" or "Intermediate+"
     * "Year-Round" → specific seasons (Winter, Spring, Summer, Fall)
     * "Scenic Views" → specific views (Glacier Views, Ocean Vistas, etc.)
  4. Effort: 2–4 hours
  5. Owner: Content team
