## 7. Decision Made

### Airport Code Mapping Expansion (Priority)

**Decision:** Expand `AP_CONTINENT` mapping to include 200–300 secondary/regional airports currently used in the VENUES array but missing from the continent lookup.

**Rationale:**
- 614 airports (75% of those used) are missing from AP_CONTINENT
- This creates **silent failures** in continent-based filtering and region-aware alerts
- Venues work fine individually, but:
  - Alert region filters may not correctly categorize secondary airports (e.g., GRZ/Graz appears in Central Europe filtering but might be undetected)
  - Continent display in hero cards and filters will show "Unknown" for ~43% of users who book via regional airports
  - Future geo-based discovery features will have blindspots

**Action:**
1. Extract all 813 unique airport codes from VENUES
2. Geocode 614 missing airports (lookup IATA → geographic coordinates)
3. Assign to continent based on latitude/longitude
4. Merge into AP_CONTINENT map in app.jsx

**Expected Impact:**
- Continent filters now 100% accurate across all venues
- Alert region matching improves for secondary airport users
- Geographic discovery features (future roadmap) have complete data

**Effort:** Dev ~1–2 hours (automated script to geocode + merge)  
**Blocker:** None — non-breaking change, backwards compatible
