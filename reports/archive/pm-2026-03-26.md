## One Strategic Risk

**The app has no rate-limiting or abuse protection on the Travelpayouts proxy.** The VPS endpoint at `peakly-api.duckdns.org` is publicly exposed (visible in DevTools on any user's session). A malicious actor could:
1. Discover the endpoint URL
2. Hit it with 1,000 requests/minute
3. Exhaust Travelpayouts monthly API quota in hours
4. Break flight pricing for all real users silently

**Mitigation:** Add Referer header check + per-IP rate limit (10 req/min) on the VPS Node.js server. 20-minute change. Recommended before any viral traffic event. Not critical for first week at 500 DAU, but necessary before hitting 10K DAU or any HN/Twitter mention.
