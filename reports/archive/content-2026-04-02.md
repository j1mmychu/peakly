## Issues Found

### Critical: AP_CONTINENT Coverage Gap (654 airports missing)

654 of 813 venue airport codes have no entry in AP_CONTINENT. The fallback in `getFlightDeal()` defaults missing airports to `"europe"`, which produces wildly inaccurate price estimates for venues in Asia, Oceania, Africa, and Latin America. Example: a venue in Bali (DPS) with no AP_CONTINENT entry gets priced as if it's in Europe.

**Impact:** Thousands of venues showing misleading estimated flight prices.
**Fix:** Bulk-add the 654 missing airport codes to AP_CONTINENT with correct continent values.

### High: AIRPORT_CITY Coverage Gap (686 airports missing)

686 of 813 venue airport codes have no entry in AIRPORT_CITY. When users search or view flight details, these airports show raw IATA codes instead of city names (e.g., "DPS" instead of "Denpasar").

**Impact:** Poor UX for the majority of venues.

### Medium: BASE_PRICES Coverage Gap (739 airports missing)

739 of 813 venue airport codes have no specific entry in BASE_PRICES. These rely on the region-based fallback in `getFlightDeal()`, which is acceptable by design — but combined with the AP_CONTINENT gap above, many estimates are doubly inaccurate.

### Low: Rating Distribution Skew

**Suggestion:** Post-launch, consider widening the rating range to 3.8–4.95 to create meaningful differentiation.
