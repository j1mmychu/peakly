## Issues Found

### CRITICAL — Revenue Blocker

**Travelpayouts marker still placeholder (line 5316).** `TP_MARKER = "YOUR_TP_MARKER"` — every flight click earns $0. This was flagged yesterday and is still not fixed. Jack must replace with real marker before Reddit posts go live.

### HIGH — 654 airports unmapped in AP_CONTINENT

Out of 813 unique airport codes used by venues, **654 (80%) have no continent mapping**. This means 1,832 venues show as "UNMAPPED" in region filters — nearly half the dataset. The Alert region filter is broken for these venues.

Continent distribution with current mappings:
- UNMAPPED: 1,832 (49%)
- North America: 705 (19%)
- Europe: 549 (15%)
- Asia: 214 (6%)
- Oceania: 208 (6%)
- Africa: 136 (4%)
- Latin America: 82 (2%)

**Fix:** Extend AP_CONTINENT with the 654 missing airport codes. This is a data task, not a code change.

### HIGH — 686 airports missing from AIRPORT_CITY

Similar gap — 686 airport codes used by venues have no city name mapping. Flight search results show raw airport codes instead of city names for these venues.

### MEDIUM — 142 duplicate photo URLs across 1,607 venues

142 unique Unsplash URLs are shared by multiple venues. Worst offenders: 5 photo URLs each used by 17 different venues. That's 43% of venues showing a photo that belongs to a different spot. Users will notice — especially if they browse multiple venues in the same category.

### MEDIUM — 132 duplicate title+location combos

132 venue entries share the same title AND location. Some are cross-category (valid — e.g., "Raja Ampat" appears in diving, kayak, fishing, tanning). But many are same-category duplicates:
- "Punta de Lobos" (Pichilemu, Chile): 3 surfing entries
- "Anchor Point" (Agadir, Morocco): 3 surfing entries
- "Whistler Blackcomb" (BC, Canada): 2 skiing entries
- "Revelstoke Mountain" (BC, Canada): 2 skiing entries
- "Clearwater Beach" (Florida): 2 tanning + 2 kite entries

Same-category duplicates should be deduplicated.

### MEDIUM — Rating compression

Ratings cluster unrealistically high:
- 4.9–5.0: 1,993 venues (53%)
- 4.8: 968 venues (26%)
- 4.5–4.7: 765 venues (21%)
- Below 4.5: 0

Average rating: 4.83. No venue below 4.5. This makes ratings meaningless as a differentiator. Real-world venue ratings should spread from ~3.5 to 4.9. Consider redistributing to a more natural curve post-launch.

### LOW — Tag inconsistency

"Year-Round" (hyphenated) and "Year Round" (no hyphen) both appear across venues. Should standardize to one format.

### LOW — "Beautiful" tag still present

2 venues use the bare tag "Beautiful" — generic and adds no value. Replace with specific descriptors.
