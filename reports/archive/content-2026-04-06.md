## 3. Airport Infrastructure Gap — NEW FINDING

Today's audit surfaced a significant airport data coverage issue:

| Map | Coverage | Missing |
|-----|----------|---------|
| AP_CONTINENT | 163 / 813 airports (20%) | 650 missing |
| AIRPORT_CITY | 179 / 813 airports (22%) | 634 missing |
| BASE_PRICES | 84 / 813 airports (10.3%) | 729 missing |

**Impact by severity:**

- **BASE_PRICES (CRITICAL):** 729 airports fall back to region-based pricing. Flight cost estimates off by $50–200+ for long-haul routes. Deal scoring (getDealScore) fundamentally unreliable for ~90% of venues.
- **AIRPORT_CITY (HIGH):** 634 airports show raw IATA codes ("BIQ") instead of city names ("Biarritz") in the UI. Poor UX for search and booking flows.
- **AP_CONTINENT (MEDIUM):** 650 airports can't be filtered by region in Alerts. Alert region filter broken for ~80% of venues.

**Recommendation:** Backfill AIRPORT_CITY and AP_CONTINENT using free IATA database (ourairports.com CSV). BASE_PRICES requires distance-based modeling or historical data — higher effort but highest impact on revenue accuracy.
