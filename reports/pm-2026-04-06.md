**Venue duplicates confirmed worse than reported.** Content agent flagged 3 venues; I found the full picture:

- **Teahupo'o**: 3 entries (`teahupoo`, `teahupo-tahiti`, `teahupoo-s141`). The `-s141` duplicate is tagged "Beach Break, All Levels" — factually dangerous misinformation for what is arguably the world's most lethal wave.
- **Cloudbreak**: 4 entries (`cloudbreak`, `cloudbreak-fiji-s21`, `cloudbreak-s85`, `cloudbreak-fiji-south-s200`). The `-s21` entry says "Beach Break, All Levels, Longboard Friendly" — it's a heavy reef break accessible only by boat.
- **Mundaka**: 3 entries (`mundaka`, `mundaka-s37`, `mundaka-left-s159`). The `-s37` entry says "Beginner Friendly, Warm Water, Year-Round" — Mundaka is cold, expert-only, and seasonal.

These batch-generated entries (`-s##` IDs) have wrong tags AND near-identical coordinates to the hand-curated originals. This means alert matching, favorites deduplication, and "similar venues" all break silently.
